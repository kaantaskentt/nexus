# Stage 3: the CEO call (v0.4)

Human-led (Kaan, with Emre where possible), about 30 minutes, with the senior stakeholder. The only stage a human runs live. v0.4 folds in the second review call (heuristic rename, people-read capture), the spine coverage audit (boundaries, sign-off criteria, artifact ask, checkpoints), the people-map branch, and example dialogues per phase.

## Objective

Stage 3 exists to get the senior stakeholder talking enough that the compiler can extract pain, names, beliefs, process boundaries, tools, vocabulary, sensitivities, and success criteria as tagged claim records. It selects which work to investigate; it never resolves what is true. The call is done when the transcript contains the record types below, in any order, by any route.

## Required record types (exit conditions)

| We need | Feeds |
|---|---|
| 2-3 real pain symptoms, at least one told as a story | Areas to Investigate |
| What they already tried with AI, and what it actually produced | Skepticism read + activation pitch qualification |
| Names of who actually does the work, plus the exec's read on each (invited deliberately, quarantined always) | Suggested People + personas + read cross-reference |
| How the CEO believes one process works, plus a time estimate | Perception-gap baseline |
| Process boundaries: what triggers it, what finished looks like | Interview scoping; spine slots 2 and 7 |
| The exec's own sign-off checks, wherever he is the approval step | Workflow success criteria (firsthand grade) |
| The real tool stack, especially the shadow one (Excel, WhatsApp) | Knowledge base + compile target |
| Their internal words, captured verbatim | Interviewer vocabulary |
| Who to talk to, what to avoid, sensitivities | NEVER list + sequencing + handling notes |
| What winning looks like in his words, who runs the result, and one real artifact committed | Demo script + handover plan + example bank |

## Key v0.4 additions over v0.3

- **Deliberate read capture (Phase 4):** "How do you read the team generally? Who is solid, who is coasting, who would surprise me?" — stored as a testable hypothesis about HIM as much as them, quarantined, cross-referenced later; floor contradicting his read becomes a capability-discovery finding, never "you were wrong."
- **Sign-off criteria question (Phase 2):** "When it lands on your desk for sign-off, what do you actually check?" — wherever he is the approval step, his checks ARE the workflow's success criteria, firsthand grade.
- **Boundaries clauses (Phase 3):** "What kicks the whole thing off? And what does finished look like?" — spine slots 2 and 7, scopes the interview.
- **The artifact ask (Phase 5):** "Before we sit with [name], could someone send us the last real one, exactly as it went out?" — commits one artifact and captures the exec's authorization for employees to share work artifacts later. Chase within 24 hours; it decays fast.
- **Checkpoints each phase:** one-breath playback, let him fix it; corrections are the most reliable records.
- **Never ask what the scrape already told us:** spend recon knowledge to buy insider trust, never recite it back.
- **Example dialogues per phase** (Ece/Bee Goddess worked examples with record-type annotations) — see the Drive original for the full dialogues.
- **The people-map branch:** triggered when the exec cannot name who does the work ("ask Meltem, she runs Izmir"). An intermediate 10-15 min interview with the named person (line manager preferred over HR), scope strictly the people map (who does what, handoffs, tenure, reads, sensitivities, sequencing) using Phase 4's questions only. Human-led during the services phase, agent-led later (passing the Stage 6 gate like any interview). Records tagged CLAIMED, reads quarantined. Branch closes when each target process has a named primary interviewee with a read. Schedule before any interview plan generates.

## Phases (with soft budgets)

1. **Warm up (~4 min).** Exit: narrating freely + AI history captured.
2. **Pain (~8 min).** Exit: one real story with names and tools + his sign-off checks where he approves.
3. **Belief (~6 min).** Exit: one process end to end, with boundaries and a time estimate. "As far as you know" gives permission to be wrong.
4. **People (~8 min).** Exit: primary interviewee named with a read, adjacent names, the exec's team read, NEVER list.
5. **Close (~4 min).** Exit: success sentence verbatim, handover reality, one artifact committed, next step agreed. Flex probe if time: brand-voice rules for content workflows.

## After the call

Upload the transcript; compiler extracts 60+ records, runs the required-fields check against the exit table; missing types become 2-3 same-day WhatsApp follow-ups. Chase the committed artifact within 24 hours. Stage 2 heuristics scored (confirmed/busted/partial), credited only when raised unprompted. If the people-map branch opened, schedule it before any interview plan generates.
